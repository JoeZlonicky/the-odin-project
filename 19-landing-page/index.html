Trees are awesome
Did you know that trees are cooler than people? Think about it. Have humans had a net-positive impact on the planet? Would you want to walk through a forest of humans? The answer to both is no. Checkmate.
Some especially cool trees
Maple trees look beautiful in the Fall and also produce maple syrup!
Willow trees have amazing trunks and can also point you to water!
Oak trees can grow to immense sizes and produce so many acorns!
Pine trees are very aromatic and their needles are a great source of vitamin C!
