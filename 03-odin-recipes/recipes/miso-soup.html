Ingredients

  • Miso paste
  • Tofu (cubed)
  • Water
  • Edamame
  • Nori (cut into strips)

Steps

  1. Bring water and miso paste to a boil in a small pot.
  2. Add tofu, and edamame to the pot and let simmer for 5 minutes.
  3. Serve in a bowl with nori strips garnished on top.
  4. Enjoy!
