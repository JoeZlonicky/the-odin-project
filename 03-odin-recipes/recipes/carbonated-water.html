Ingredients

  • Water
  • CO2
  • Lemon or lime juice (optional)

Steps

  1. Fill SodaStream bottle with water to the specified line.
  2. Insert into SodaStream machine and use your preferred carbonation setting.
  3. Remove from machine and pour into a glass.
  4. Optional: add some lemon or lime juice.
  5. Enjoy!
